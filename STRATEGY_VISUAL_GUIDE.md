**Select based on your market outlook:**

### ?? BULLISH
You expect price to go **UP**

Shows strategies:
- Long Call
- Bull Call Spread
- Synthetic Long

### ?? BEARISH
You expect price to go **DOWN**

Shows strategies:
- Long Put
- Bear Call Spread
- Bear Put Spread

### ?? NEUTRAL
You expect price to stay **SAME** or **UNCERTAIN**

Shows strategies:
- Short Call
- Short Put
- Straddle
- Iron Condor

## Strategy Selection Examples

### Example 1: Bullish on NIFTY

```
STEP 1: Market View
        Select: ?? Bullish

STEP 2: Available Strategies
        • ?? Long Call (Buy 1 Call)
        • ?? Bull Spread (Buy+Sell)
        • ?? Synthetic (Very bullish)

STEP 3: Choose Based on Confidence
        Low confidence ? Bull Spread (Low risk)
        Medium confidence ? Long Call (Medium risk)
        High confidence ? Synthetic (High risk)

STEP 4: Say you choose Long Call
        Read details:
        - Entry: Buy 1 Call at ATM
        - Risk: Medium (premium paid)
        - Reward: High (unlimited)

STEP 5: Look at CALLS table
        Find ATM call (close to ?25,000)
        Check volume and spread
        Click [BUY]

STEP 6: Full modal opens
        Shows all analysis
        Shows decision criteria
        Confirm if match

STEP 7: Execute!
```

## Tips & Tricks

### ?? Quick Strategy Selection
1. Know your market view first
2. Browse all options for that view
3. Choose based on your risk tolerance
4. Read the details
5. Confirm with table data

### ?? Combine with Table Data
- Strategy says "Buy ATM Call"
- Check CALLS table
- Find the ATM row
- Verify volume is good
- Verify spread is tight
- Then buy with confidence!

### ?? Learning Path
1. Start with simple strategies (Long Call/Put)
2. Try spreads (Bull/Bear spreads)
3. Explore neutral strategies
4. Master complex strategies
5. Develop your own!

### ?? Risk Management
- Always check "Max Loss" in details
- Make sure you can afford it
- Use spreads to limit risk
- Check IV before buying/selling
- Check volume before entry
