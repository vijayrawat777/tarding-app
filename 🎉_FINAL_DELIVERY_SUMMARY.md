## ?? HOW TO USE (In 60 Seconds)

### Step-by-Step
```
1. Open /option-chain in browser
2. Look at LEFT sidebar (?? TRADING STRATEGIES)
3. Select Market View (Bullish/Bearish/Neutral)
4. Browse strategy cards (they appear!)
5. Click the strategy you like (it highlights blue)
6. Read full details in panel below
7. Look at option chain tables (Calls/Puts)
8. Find strikes matching the strategy
9. Click [BUY] or [SELL] on any strike
10. Full analysis modal opens
11. Read all the decision info
12. Click Confirm
13. Done! Order executed!
```
