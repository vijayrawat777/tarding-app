## ?? Complete Workflow Example

```
SCENARIO: Bullish on NIFTY50, expect +200 points in 1 week

  Stock: NIFTY50
  Expiry: 24 Jan 2024 (1 week)
  ?

  ?? Bullish Card shows:
     - Call volume: 250K (higher than puts)
     - Call OI: 2.5M
     - Avg Call IV: 22.5%
  ? Confirms bullish setup
  ?

STEP 3: CHOOSE STRATEGY
  Current View ? Strategy Builder
  ? Click "Bullish Strategy" button
  ? See Long Call strategy
  ? Max Profit: Unlimited
  ? Max Loss: Premium Paid
  ? Matches your outlook
  ?

STEP 4: ANALYZE GREEKS
  View ? Greeks Analysis
  ? Spot price: ?25000
  ? Look at ATM call (25000 strike)
  ? Delta: 0.50 = moves ?0.50 per ?1 stock move
  ? Gamma: 0.0018 = delta accelerating nicely
  ? Theta: -0.12 = loses ?0.12 per day (time decay)
  ? Good for directional play
  ?

STEP 5: REVIEW PAYOFF
  View ? Payoff Chart
  ? See Long Call payoff
  ? Loss = premium paid (?50)
  ? Profit = unlimited above (strike + premium)
  ? Confirm risk matches account
  ? Ready to trade
  ?

STEP 6: SELECT STRIKE
  View ? Option Chain
  ? Filter Strategy: OTM (to reduce cost)
  ? See 25100 CE at ?40 (cheaper than 50)
  ? Delta: 0.65 (good directional exposure)
  ? Volume: High (easy to exit)
  ? Selected
  ?

STEP 7: PLACE ORDER
  ? Click [BUY] on 25100 CE
  ? Confirm quantity
  ? Confirm price ?40
  ? SET STOP LOSS: ?30 (25% loss)
  ? SET TARGET: ?60 (50% profit)
  ? Order Placed!
  ?

STEP 8: MONITOR
  ? Watch delta changes
  ? Monitor theta decay daily
  ? Exit at stop loss or target
  ? Don't hold to expiration
  ? Trade Complete
```

## ?? Color Guide

```
SIGNAL COLORS:
?? Green  ? Bullish signals, Good news, BUY action
?? Red    ? Bearish signals, Bad news, SELL action
?? Purple ? Neutral signals, Balanced, Caution
?? Orange ? Warnings, High IV, Caution levels

TABLE COLORS:
Cyan (#00d4ff)  ? Active elements, primary info
Green (#00ff88) ? Positive movement, ITM calls
Red (#ff6b6b)   ? Negative movement, ITM puts
Gold (#ffd700)  ? Strike prices (scanning focus)
Dark Blue       ? Main background

RISK BADGES:
?? Low     ? Less risky, conservative (delta < 0.3)
?? Medium  ? Moderate risk, balanced (delta 0.3-0.7)
?? High    ? Risky, aggressive (delta > 0.7)
```
