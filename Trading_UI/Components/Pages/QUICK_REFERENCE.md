# Option Chain Page - Quick Reference Card

## ?? One-Page Decision Guide

### BULLISH (Stock will go UP)
```
? BUY CALLS       - High profit potential, limited loss
? SELL PUTS       - Collect premium, stock can rise
? BULL SPREAD     - Lower cost, limited risk/reward
```
**Best Signals**: High Call Volume, Low PCR (<0.7), Rising IV

### BEARISH (Stock will go DOWN)
```
? BUY PUTS        - High profit potential, limited loss
? SELL CALLS      - Collect premium, stock can fall
? BEAR SPREAD     - Lower cost, limited risk/reward
```
**Best Signals**: High Put Volume, High PCR (>1.3), Rising IV

### NEUTRAL (Stock will stay SIDEWAYS)
```
? IRON CONDOR     - Profit from time decay, limited risk
? STRADDLE        - Profit from big moves in any direction
? BUTTERFLIES     - Limited risk, profits in range
```
**Best Signals**: High IV, PCR near 1.0, Range-bound price action

| Strategy | Strike Type | Reasoning |

| Sell Call (Bearish) | OTM | More premium, less likely ITM |
| Sell Put (Bullish) | OTM | More premium, less likely ITM |

## ?? When to BUY vs SELL

### BUY When:
- ? You have directional conviction
- ? IV is low (options cheap)
- ? Time value is high (days to expiry)
- ? After market decline (fear = low prices)
- ? Before positive event/earnings

### SELL When:
- ? You're neutral or opposite direction
- ? IV is high (options expensive)
- ? Time value is low (close to expiry)
- ? After market rally (greed = high prices)
- ? Before calm expected period

## ? Time-Based Decisions

| Weeks to Expiry | Best For | Avoid |
|-----------------|----------|-------|
| 4+ weeks | Long positions, high theta | Short positions |
| 2-4 weeks | Selling, time decay | Long far OTM |
| 1-2 weeks | Selling premium heavily | Long anything |
| <1 week | Exit trades | New entries |

## ?? Risk Management Golden Rules

```
Rule 1: Never risk >2% of account per trade
Rule 2: Exit at 20-30% loss
Rule 3: Exit at 50% of max profit
Rule 4: Set stop loss BEFORE entering
Rule 5: Don't hold to expiry
Rule 6: Use smaller size for multi-leg strategies
Rule 7: Avoid selling naked short calls
Rule 8: Take profits when available
```

## ?? Warning Signs

| Warning | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Very High IV (>50%) | Avoid buying now, consider selling |
| Very Low IV (<15%) | Buy now, avoid selling |
| Price near strong resistance | Reduce bullish bets |
| Price near strong support | Reduce bearish bets |
| Upcoming earnings | Expect volatility spike |
| Low volume on strike | Avoid, hard to exit |
| Wide bid-ask spread | Avoid, will lose on spread |

## ?? Trading Philosophies

### Conservative Approach (New Traders)
- Trade ATM only
- Small position sizes
- Quick exit targets
- High win rate focus

### Moderate Approach (Experienced)
- Mix ATM and OTM
- Medium position sizes
- Defined risk strategies
- Risk/reward 1:2 minimum

### Aggressive Approach (Professional)
- Wide range of strikes
- Larger position sizes
- Multi-leg strategies
- Portfolio Greeks management
