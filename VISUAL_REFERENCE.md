## ? QUICK DECISION GUIDES

### SHOULD I BUY THIS CALL?

```
Check:
1. ? Is bid-ask spread reasonable?
   (Ask - Bid) should be ? 5% of price

2. ? Is volume high enough?
   3.0M+ is good, <500K is risky

3. ? What's my directional view?
   Bullish? Buy call ?
   Bearish? Buy put ?
   Neutral? Avoid ?

4. ? Can I afford the loss?
   Max loss = LTP price
   Is it within your 2% rule?

5. ? What's the Delta?
   OTM: High risk, low cost
   ATM: Medium risk, medium cost
   ITM: Low risk, high cost

If all ? ? BUY [B]
If any ? ? SKIP
```

### SHOULD I SELL THIS CALL?

```
Check:
1. ? Is IV high?
   High IV = collect more premium ?

2. ? Is the bid-ask spread tight?
   Easy to exit if needed

3. ? What's my directional view?
   Neutral? Sell ?
   Bearish? Sell ?
   Bullish? Avoid ?

4. ? Is volume good?
   Easy to exit position

5. ? Do you have margin?
   Required for selling

If all ? ? SELL [S]
If any ? ? SKIP
```
